About Salamia

Spreading Islamic Greetings and Blessings

Salamia is a platform dedicated to helping Muslims share beautiful Islamic greetings and prayers with their loved ones. In our busy lives, we sometimes forget to express our faith-based well-wishes, but with Salamia, you can create and share meaningful cards in seconds.

Our collection includes authentic Muslim prayers and greetings for various occasions - from Ramadan and Eid to everyday blessings. Each card combines beautiful design with heartfelt messages to help you connect with your community.

Whether you're wishing someone a blessed Friday, congratulating them on Eid, or offering condolences, Salamia makes it easy to share Islamic greetings that come from the heart.

Frequently Asked Questions

What occasions can I create cards for?+

You can create cards for Ramadan, Eid, Jummah (Friday), Hajj, condolences, and general duas (prayers). Each occasion has a selection of appropriate Islamic greetings and prayers.

Are the messages authentic Islamic greetings?+

Yes, all messages are common Islamic prayers and greetings that Muslims use in daily life. They are authentic expressions of faith that are culturally appropriate for each occasion.

Can I customize the cards?+

Yes, you can choose from 13 different templates, add your name as the sender, and generate different messages until you find one that resonates with you.

How do I share my card?+

You can download the card as an image to share on social media or messaging apps, or you can generate a shareable link that opens directly to the card view.

Is Salamia free to use?+

Yes, Salamia is completely free to use. Our mission is to help spread Islamic greetings and blessings without any cost barriers.
